Basic BaseOperation classes
-----------------------------
Basic BaseOperation classes do simple retrievals. Their input and output are `xarray.Dataset <http://xarray.pydata.org/en/stable/generated/xarray.Dataset.html>`_ and `addict.Dict <https://github.com/mewwts/addict>`_.

* :class:`ELDAmwl.extinction_factories.SlopeToExtinctionDefault`
* :class:`ELDAmwl.extinction_factories.ExtinctionAutosmoothDefault`
* :class:`ELDAmwl.signals.CombineDepolComponentsDefault`
